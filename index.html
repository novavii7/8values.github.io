What is KinkValues?

KinkValues is, in essence, a quiz that attempts to assign percentages for eight different sexual values. You will be presented by a statement, and then you will answer with your opinion on the statement, from Strongly Agree to Strongly Disagree, with each answer slightly affecting your scores. At the end of the quiz, your answers will be compared to the maximum possible for each value, thus giving you a percentage. Answer honestly!

There are questions in the test.

What are the eight values?

There are four independent axes - Power, Position, Pleasure, and Kink - and each has two opposing values assigned to them. They are:

DOMINANT

Those with higher dominance scores enjoy playing the dominant role and controlling their partner.
